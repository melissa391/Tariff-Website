About


When the UK is no longer in the European Union, businesses will have to pay tariffs (trade taxes) in order to export food products and other goods to the remaining EU countries, unless a bilateral trade deal is agreed. If there is a ‘no deal’ Brexit, World Trade Organisation (WTO) tariff rates, on EU exports, will come into effect. The data on this website is taken from WTO’s tariff database.
